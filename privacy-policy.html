Introduction

MindFlow ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our journaling and mood tracking application.

Information We Collect

None. MindFlow does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

What This Means:

  • ✅ 100% Offline: All your journal entries and mood data are stored locally on your device
  • ✅ No Servers: We do not operate any servers or cloud services
  • ✅ No Tracking: We do not track your usage, location, or behavior
  • ✅ No Third Parties: We do not share data with any third-party services
  • ✅ No Internet Required: The app works completely offline

Data Storage

All data created in MindFlow, including:

  • Journal entries
  • Mood tracking data
  • Settings and preferences

...is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (Hive database and SharedPreferences).

Your Data is Safe:

  • Data never leaves your device
  • No internet connection is required or used
  • You have complete control over your data
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all your data

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your device's security features protect your data
  • We recommend using device lock (PIN, password, fingerprint, etc.)
  • Keep your device updated with the latest security patches

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Individual Entries: Delete any journal entry from within the app
  • All Data: Use the "Clear All Data" option in Settings
  • Complete Removal: Uninstall the app to remove all data

Children's Privacy

MindFlow does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.
